CLUB ZERO GRAVITY

Collaborate Learn Innovate

Zero Gravity is the official club of Information Technology Department of Smt. Kashibai Navale College of Engineering. We aim to collaborate, learn and innovate new things. If you want to join us, kindly drop us a mail or meet us at our department.

Ishwar Sarade
Founder-President,

OUR MAGAZINE

Zero Gravity is an E-zine started by the students of IT Department of SKNCOE. This magazine aims to showcase the energy and euphoria that runs across the campus during various events.It also provides you a bulk of technical hacks and articles. It highlights stories of the students who endeavor to achieve success in extra curricular activities. This magazine also has a corner for students for submitting their artwork and confessions.

Lubna Shaikh
Editor,
Club Zero Gravity.
